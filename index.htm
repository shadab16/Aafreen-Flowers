Introduction

A flower, sometimes known as a bloom or blossom, is the reproductive structure found in flowering plants.

In addition to facilitating the reproduction of flowering plants, flowers have long been admired and used by humans to beautify their environment, but also as objects of rituals, religion, medicine and as a source of food.

Many flowers have important symbolic meanings in Western culture. The practice of assigning meanings to flowers is known as floriography.
